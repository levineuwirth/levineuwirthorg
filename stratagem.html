STRATAGEM
A game with unbelievably rich strategy

General Release: 2025

I've always had ideas for improvements to my favorite grand strategy games. Now that I have enough computer science knowledge to undertake a massive project, I'm going to create the game I've always wanted to play.

Stratagem is a game that emphasizes, you guessed it, strategy. This game will be similar to the games of Paradox Interactive, and will take you from prehistory to an intergalactic future, all with an emphasis on strategy on every level!
