Symphony no. 3

Completed: September 2022

My third symphony, in five minutes for full orchestral forces, is the longest piece I have ever written. The piece is a personal declaration and is deeply connected with my musical roots, being the first piece I completed as an adult.

Please contact me with inquiries about performances.
